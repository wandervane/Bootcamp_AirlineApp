Baggage Security Reminder

1. All liquids must be in containers of no     more than 3.4 ounces or 100ml, and all     containers must fit in a clear, one quart
    sized zip top plastic bag.

2. Items in carry-on baggage may not
    exceed three ounces

3. Items in checked baggage may not
    exceed 16 ounces per container or 70
    ounces total per passenger.

Items not allowed in carry-on

These are the items not allowed in your carry-on bags :

1. Liquids, creams, gels and pastes in
    containers over 100ml.

2. Nail cutters, Swiss knives, scissors, any
    bladed items or sharp objects.

3. Umbrellas of any type

4. Bicycle chains and jacks or other similar
    items.

5. Lighters, which contain “butane” as well
     as matches.
